How to study

  • Read the BPP workshop for the topic first
  • Drill MCQs immediately after — click any topic in the sidebar
  • Read every explanation, including for correct answers
  • Tick the checkbox when fully done
  • Check Weak Spots weekly and return to BPP material only for those

Exam format

  • Criminal: 75 MCQs, 3 hrs, closed book — 16 April
  • Civil Paper 1: Closed book — 21 April
  • Civil Paper 2: Open book (White Book) — 23 April
  • Every question is scenario-based — you are always in role
  • Four options — pick the single BEST answer
  • No negative marking — attempt every question
